About

I have had so many wonderful opportunities since I started this career over 16 years ago. I love getting to know new people and engaging with my clients. They truly become my friends and family. I wouldn’t have it any other way! I am a mother of two children, and a teenage step-daughter. I know what it’s like to be busy, because of that my goal is to make each client feel stylish, while still having a look they can maintain on a daily basis, without me! I offer almost every service from color, men’s services, women’s services, even perms and relaxers. I rarely have openings on weekends or evenings because I’m completely booked but if that’s what you need you can always text me for last minute openings. I DO have day time spots open. I offer a 10-minute color. That allows you to get a single color applied, rinsed, and styled in a hour if you want to take a lunch break for a hair service. Message me for details.

Public service announcement.
