About

Hey! Thank you for stopping by my website. I’m Cozzette, a hairstylist for over 10 years, and I’m loving every minute of it. A lot has changed since I started. I have owned three salons: Studio 110, BB 63 Salon, and BB 63 Beauty Parlor (in Northside). I have managed over 15 stylists, paid tons of bills, and was darn good at it, but three years ago things changed for me. I had a baby! I wanted some of my personal time back, and I wanted to put my clients first again, so I closed my store fronts.

I now have a few stylists I love and trust from the old shop who work with me, so BB 63 Salon is a small version of what it once was, but it’s still awesome. Running a salon is great, but it’s a never-ending job, and I was getting tired of the extra stress, so I moved to a suite in the Camp Bowie area in July 2017 and haven’t looked back.

I have continued my education with many lines, including DevaCurl, CHI, aquage, Erayba, and many more. I also offer body-wave perms on medium-length hair and above. At this point in my life, I want to minimize stress and put my focus on my salon guests and their needs, then return home to my family and make them my priority. I now have two children, and we know how fast they grow up!

For your convenience, I offer 24/7 online booking, and you can text me anytime with any questions you may have, and I will return the message in a timely manner. For more info see the icons below.
